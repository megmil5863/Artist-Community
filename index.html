How to Submit Artwork

We welcome submissions from artists! Please follow these steps:

  1. Prepare your artwork in a digital format (JPEG, PNG, etc.).
  2. Include a brief description of your work.
  3. Email your submission to your-email@example.com.
  4. We will review your submission and get back to you!
